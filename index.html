Welcome to The School of the Unknown Website

Glebe is an inclusive school where pupils, parents, staff, governors, the local authority and local services work in partnership to meet the needs of our diverse community.

We aim to:

  • inspire an enthusiasm for learning,
  • make a difference to the lives of all learners
  • build lasting memories

At The School of the Unknown the children and their families have access to a wide range of additional services including out of hours study sessions, family learning, health and social care support and additional sport and arts opportunities. We celebrate the positive contribution made by every member of our school. Our curriculum is inspiring and we have a sharp focus on meeting the individual needs of all our learners.
